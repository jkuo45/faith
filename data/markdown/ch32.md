1. Happy 😃 is the man who hath sown in his breast the seeds of benevolence: the produce thereof shall be clarity and love.
    1. “Happy is the man”, “Paradise” is heavily influenced by the Koran[?]
    2. Some texts might use the term “gay” [Professional Conduct]
2. From the fountain ⛲️ of his heart ❤️ shall rise rivers of goodness; and the streams shall overflow, for the benefit of mankind.
3. He [assists] the poor in their trouble; he rejoices in furthering the prosperity of all men.
4. He does not disapprove of his neighbor [He loves his neighbor]. He does not believe the tales of envy and malevolence. He never repeats their slanders.
    1. [jk: my bad on this one for real]
5. He forgives the injuries of men, he wipes them from his memory. Revenge and malice have no place in his heart.
6. He does not return evil 😈 with evil 👿. He does not hate his enemies, but requiteth (repay) their injustice with a friendly admonition.

7. The griefs and anxieties of men excite his compassion; he endeavors to alleviate the weight of their misfortunes, and the pleasure of success rewards his labor.
8. He calms the fury, he heals the quarrels of angry men, and prevents the mischiefs of strife and animosity.
9. He promotes in his neighborhood peace and good will, and his name is repeated with praise and benedictions.
