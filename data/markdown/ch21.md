# Chapter 21: Marriage Instructions for Man and Wife

---

[https://hermetic.com/moorish/7koran#chapter-xxi](https://hermetic.com/moorish/7koran#chapter-xxi)

1. Daughters of God listen well to instructions of good sense/judgement and let the law sink deep in thy heart so shall the charms of your mind add luster to the elegance of your form (body). Any thy beauty, like thy rose it resembles, shall retain its sweetness when its bloom is withered.
2. In the spring of thy youth, in the morning of thy days, when the eyes 👀 of men gaze on thee with delight 😍, and nature whispereth in thine ear the meaning of their looks. Hear with caution ⚠️ their seducing words; guard well thy heart, nor listen to their soft persuasion.
3. Remember thou art made man's reasonable companion, the slave of his passion; the purpose of your being is not merely to gratify his loose desires but to help him in work (toil) of life, to soothe his heart with thy tenderness and compensate this care with soft endearments 😊.
4. Win his heart by subduing him to love and reign in his heart ♥️.
5. One should walk with maiden sweetness, with innocence 🕊️ in her mind; and modesty on her cheek.
6. She keeps her hands busy.

Chapter 21 *Marriage Instructions for Man and Woman from the Noble Prophet*

1. She is dothed with neatness, she is fed with temperance, humility, and meekness as a crown of glory circling her head 😇.
2. On her tongue dwelleth music , the sweetness of honey 🍯 floweth from her lips 👄. Decency is in all her words, in her answers are mildness and truth.
3. Respect and obedience are the lessons of her life, and peace and happiness are her reward.
4. Before her steps walketh prudence and virtue attendeth at her right hand.
5. Her eye speaketh softness and love, but sharp discretion sits on her brow. 
6. Her virtue keeps the abandoned silent.
7. The finger of silence rests on her lips, especially when scandal is busy. She speaks openly about charity and good nature, especially if the fame is tossed from tongue to tongue.
8. Her heart is the home of goodness, and therefore she does not suspect evil of others.
9. Happy is the man that should make her his wife; happy is the child that should call her mother.
10. She presides in the house , and there is peace; she commands with judgement 🇹🇼, and is obeyed.
11. She ariseth (wake) in the morning , she considers her affairs, and appointeth to every one of their proper business.
12. The care of her family is her whole delight 😊; to that alone she applieth her study; and elegance with frugality is seen in her house.
13. The prudence of her management is an honor to her husband, he heareth her praise with secret delight 🤗.

Chapter 21 v.7-19

1. She informeth the minds of her children with wisdom 📚; she fashions their manners from the examples of her own goodness.
2. The words of her mouth is the law of their youth ; the motion of her eye commands their obedience.
3. She speaketh 🗣️, and the servants fly; she pointeth 👉, and the thing is done ✅; for the law of love is in their hearts, and her kindness addeth wings to their feet.
4.  In prosperity she is not puffed up; in adversity she heals the wounds of fortune with patience.
5.  The troubles of her husband are alleviated by her councils and sweetened by her endearments; he puts his heart in her bosom and receives comfort ❤️‍🩹.
6. Happy is the man that has made her his wife; happy is the child that call her mother.
