# Chapter 20: Holy Instructions and Warnings for All Young Men

---

[https://hermetic.com/moorish/7koran#chapter-xx](https://hermetic.com/moorish/7koran#chapter-xx)

1. Beware of all the allurements of wantonness (lust[?]) and do not let the harlot tempt thee to excess with her delight.
2. The madness 😛 of desire shall defeat its own pursuits from the blindness of its rage 😡, thou shalt rush upon destruction.
3. ⚠️ Therefore give not up thy heart to her sweet enticements neither suffer thy soul to be enslaved by her enchanting delusions.
4. The fountain of health ⛲️💪 which must supply the stream of pleasure shall quickly be dried up, and every spring of joy shall be exhausted.
5. In the prime of thy old age shall overtake thee; the sun shall decline the morning of thy days.
6. But when virtue and modesty enlighten her charms, the luster of a beautiful woman is brighter than the stars of Heaven and the influence of her power, it is in vain to resist.

Chapter 20 Holy Instructions and Warnings for All Young Men v.1-6

1. The pureness of her heart transcends the lily 🌸; her smile is more delicious than a garden of roses 🌹🌹🌹.
2. The innocence  of her eyes 🥺 is like that of the turtle 🐢; simplicity and truth dwell in her heart.
3. The kisses  of her mouth are sweeter than honey ; the perfumes of Arabia breathe from her lips .
4. Do not shut your heart  to the tenderness of love ❤️; the purity 🕊️ of its flame 🔥 shall ennoble your heart, and soften it to receive the fairest impressions 🥺.
