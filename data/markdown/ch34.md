# Chapter 34: Sincerity

---

[https://hermetic.com/moorish/7koran#chapter-xxxiv](https://hermetic.com/moorish/7koran#chapter-xxxiv)

1. Those who are enamored with the beauty of Truth and has fixed thy heart on the simplicity of her [truth] charms, hold fast thy loyalty (fidelity) unto her [truth], and forsake her [truth] not; the constancy of thy virtue shall crown thee consistency with honor.
2. The tongue of the sincere is rooted in the heart ♥️. Hypocrisy and deceit have ❌ no place in his words 🗣️.
3. He/She has a steady eye in speaking the truth; and will blush at falsehoods.
4. He supports, as a man, the dignity of his character. To the arts of hypocrisy he scorns to stoop.
5. He is consistent with himself. He is never embarrassed. He has courage enough for truth and is afraid to lie.
    
    ![IMG_1716.jpeg](Chapter%2034%20Sincerity%20253f52c20e77817ba420ce275b6811c6/IMG_1716.jpeg)
    
6. He is far above discourse. The words of his mouth 👄 are the thoughts 💭of his heart ♥️.
7. He only opens his mouth if with caution and good sense.
8. He studies what is right and speaks with discretion 🤔.
9. He advises with friendship and corrects openly. Whatever he promises shall surely be performed 👌.
10. ❌ The heart of the hypocrite is hid in his chest. He makes his words in the semblance of truth, while the business of his life is to deceive 👺.
11. ❌ He works in the dark as a mole and thinks he is safe. When he blunders into light, he is betrayed and exposed with dirt on his hands ✋.
    
    ![IMG_1717.jpeg](Chapter%2034%20Sincerity%20253f52c20e77817ba420ce275b6811c6/IMG_1717.jpeg)
    
12. ❌ He (the insincere, the hypocrite) passes his days in perpetual constraint, his tongue and heart are forever at variance (conflicted).
13. ❌ He tries and works for the character of a righteous man; and huggeth himself in the thoughts of his community.
14. ❌ The pains which thou taketh to hide what thou art, more than would make thee what thou would seem.
